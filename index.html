About Our Mission

Founded in the aftermath of the Great Debbie Incident of 2023 (we don't talk about it), the Anti-Debbie Coalition has grown from a small support group to an international movement with thousands of members who have all had enough of Debbie's shenanigans.

Our mission is simple: document, educate, and prevent future Debbie-related incidents through awareness and solidarity.
